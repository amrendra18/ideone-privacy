How to install this extension on your chrome browser!!


Step 1: Go to chrome://extensions/ 

Step 2: Check the tick box for Developer mode if its unchecked

Step 3: Download and extract ideone_privacy-unpacked.zip from this repository.

Step 4: Then click on Load unpacked extensions and locate the extracted folder.

Step 5: You should see Ideone Privacy in your extensions now.

Step 6: Go ahead and test it :D
